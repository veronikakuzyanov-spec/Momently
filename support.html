Frequently asked questions

How fast will I be matched with an Agent?

Most bookings in Greenville are matched with a nearby Agent in under 10 minutes. If no Agent is available, you can schedule ahead instead.

When do I get my photos and video?

An AI-assisted preview typically arrives within a few minutes of your session ending. Fully edited content follows within a few hours, based on the package you booked.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel from the Bookings tab. Cancellations made with enough notice are fully refunded — see the current cancellation window in your booking confirmation.

How are Agents vetted?

Every Agent completes ID verification, a background check, and a skills review before they can accept bookings.

How do I become an Agent?

Apply from the "Become an Agent" section of the app or website. You'll submit a sample reel, complete ID verification and a background check, and finish a short orientation.

Still need help?

Email support@momently.app and we'll get back to you within one business day. For an active in-session safety concern, use the in-app SOS button — don't wait on email.
