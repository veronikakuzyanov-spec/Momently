Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Momently, Inc. ("Momently," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Momently app and website, as a customer ("Booker") or as a creator ("Agent").

1. Information We Collect

CategoryExamplesCollected from
Account informationName, email, phone number, passwordAll users
Identity & verification dataGovernment-issued ID, selfie for ID match, background-check resultsAgents only
Location dataPrecise GPS location during active bookings, general location for matchingAll users, during active bookings
Payment informationPayment card details (processed by our payment processor, not stored by us directly), payout bank detailsBookers (payment) / Agents (payout)
ContentPhotos and videos captured or uploaded through the appAll users
CommunicationsIn-app chat and voice call metadata between Booker and AgentAll users
Usage & device dataApp interactions, device type, crash logs, IP addressAll users

2. How We Use Information

  • To match Bookers with available, nearby, verified Agents.
  • To process payments and agent payouts.
  • To verify Agent identity and eligibility, including background checks, before allowing them to accept bookings.
  • To provide live tracking, in-app messaging, and safety features (including the Safety Verification Code and SOS button) during active bookings.
  • To deliver, edit, and store the photo/video content produced during a booking.
  • To send booking-related notifications, receipts, and (with consent) marketing communications.
  • To investigate safety reports, disputes, and violations of our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines.
  • To improve matching, pricing, and the AI-assisted preview and editing features.

3. How We Share Information

  • Between Booker and Agent: first name, photo, rating, live location, and chat/call access are shared between the two parties for the duration of a booking, to make the in-person meetup work.
  • Service providers: payment processing (e.g., Stripe), identity and background-check verification (e.g., Checkr), cloud hosting, and analytics providers, each bound by contract to protect your data.
  • Legal & safety: where required by law, or to investigate a safety incident, fraud, or violation of our terms.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy or a successor policy.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

4. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can access, correct, or request deletion of your account information by contacting privacy@momently.app.
  • Location access can be managed through your device settings, though disabling it will prevent booking and matching from working.
  • Depending on your state of residence, you may have additional rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) — contact us to exercise these rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain booking records, content, and identity-verification data for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal and safety obligations, and resolve disputes, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

6. Children's Privacy

Momently is not directed to, and may not be used by, anyone under the age of 18.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@momently.app or Momently, Inc., Greenville, SC.
